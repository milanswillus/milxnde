🍒 cherry chess — privacy policy

effective july 15, 2026

the short version

Cherry Chess collects no data. None. The app has no servers, no accounts, no ads, no analytics, and no third-party SDKs.

data collection

Cherry Chess does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. The app works entirely offline and never connects to the internet.

local data

Your games, training progress, and settings are stored only on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to me or anyone else. Deleting the app permanently deletes this data.

third parties

Cherry Chess contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking frameworks of any kind.

children

Since no data is collected from anyone, no data is collected from children either.

changes

If this policy ever changes (for example, if a future version adds an online feature), the update will be published on this page before it takes effect.

contact

Questions about this policy? Email support@cherrychess.milxn.de.
